You are given two strings, <b>A</b> and <b>B</b>, where the length of <b>A</b> is less than or equal to the length of <b>B</b>.  You can apply an arbitrary number of operations to <b>A</b>, where each operation is one of the following:

<ul>
<li>Choose a character c and add it to the beginning of <b>A</b>.</li>
<li>Choose a character c and add it to the end of <b>A</b>.</li>
</ul>
